About Me

Meet your new tech triple-threat. Michelle Pinsky has over six years of design experience in print, web, logo, and packaging. Michelle has spent the majority of her career in agency settings, previously working at APCO Worldwide and has catered to Fortune 50 clients. Previous clients have included the Blue Cross Blue Shield Association, Pulmuone Foods, USA, MARS Petcare, and Hilton Worldwide. In 2013, Michelle helped lead the national rebranding and repositioning design for Monterey Gourmet Foods/Wildwood, as well as generating revenue growth for products in the Isabella’s Kitchen product line in club stores such as Sam’s Club and Costco. After her cutting her chops in UI/UX design at Unboxed Technology, she enrolled in The Coding Bootcamp at the University of Richmond for full-stack web development. She graduated with a dual degree in Marketing Communications and Graphic Design from Mary Baldwin College, class of 2012.
